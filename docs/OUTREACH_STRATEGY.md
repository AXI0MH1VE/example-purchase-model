# Outreach Strategy & High-Leverage Templates (2025)

## ◈ Strategy Overview

Focus: **Quality over Quantity.**
Targeting decision-makers at premium e-commerce brands (Shopify, BigCommerce) in high-AOV niches like Baby & Child, Luxury Furniture, and Electronics.

---

## ◈ LinkedIn Template 1: The Industry Trendsetter

**Goal**: Establish expertise and identify growth caps.

> "Hi [Name], I've been monitoring the 71% AOV jump in the baby gear sector for 2025. It looks like [Company] is positioned perfectly, but I noticed a few friction points in the checkout flow that might be capping your growth. I've developed a framework specifically for high-AOV Shopify stores—would you be open to a quick chat to trade notes?"

---

## ◈ LinkedIn Template 2: The Problem Solver (Fulfillment Focused)

**Goal**: Address margin erosion due to procurement inefficiency.

> "I noticed [Company] is scaling rapidly in the luxury furniture niche. In this phase, many brands struggle with procurement efficiency. We specialize in dynamic fulfillment routing that protects margins by identifying superior wholesale terms in real-time. Thought it might be worth a 15-minute intro."

---

## ◈ Authority Building & Case Studies

- **Document Every Success**: Record precisely how the Axiom Hive engine doubled AOV or protected 5% margins for a client.
- **Outcome-Based Proof**: Publish results on LinkedIn, and in high-profile communities like **r/Entrepreneur** or **r/SaaS**.
- **The "Audit Magnet" CTA**: Always lead with the free Conversion & Margin Audit to lower the barrier for initial contact.

---

## ◈ Outreach Workflow

1. **Prospecting**: Identify 10-20 high-potential brands and their founders/CMOs.
2. **Personalized Ice-breaker**: Use Template 1 or 2 as a base, but add a specific detail from their current store UI.
3. **The Diagnostic Call**: Utilize the `AUDIT_FRAMEWORK.md` to conduct a 15-minute diagnostic.
4. **The Outcome Pitch**: Present the roadmap and the Axiom Hive engine as the solution.
